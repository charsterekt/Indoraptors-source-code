Birds of Prey

The Indoraptors project is an AI powered tool capable of identifying 109 unique species of raptors found in and around the Indian subcontinent. Simply upload an image and wait! The AI has been trained primarily on adult male specimens, and as a result female or juvenile entries may not be entirely accurate.

  • Click or drag an image to upload
  • Supported file formats include .jpg, .png and .jpeg images
  • Image file size is limited to 16 MB
  • You can only choose one image at a time
